About Us

Medinix is a high school nonprofit dedicated to improving medication adherence for seniors, individuals in rural communities, or any person in need.

Our Mission

We aim to create a world where no one misses a critical dose. Our focus is to improve health outcomes by helping individuals stick to their prescribed medication routines.

Medicine Reminder System

We are currently developing a medicine reminder and tracking system designed to help people take the correct medications at the right time and dosage. This system will send alerts and allow individuals or caregivers to track medication adherence.
